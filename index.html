Jollof Rice Recipes

Description

This traditional Nigerian dish brings out the best possible flavor of rice and chicken.

Chicken is first sauteed on the stove top to produce a wonderful aromatic base for the rice. For an extra special and authentic touch, serve jollof rice with fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Large onion
  • Water
  • Rice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
